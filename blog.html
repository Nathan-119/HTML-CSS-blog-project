1.1

Describing the Purpose of IT in modern Buisness

IT is the heart of modern businesses, it aids with communication through emails, for many companies this is the primary means of communication between employees, suppliers, and customers. Other methods of communication include live chat system, video-conferencing systems, voice over internet protocol (VOIP) programs offer more and more ways to communicate for a company, whereas before this would be limited to perhaps the countries origin, the rise of IT allows for companies to communicate easily on a global scale within minutes. In addition to this, data handling is also made simple thanks to IT through digital storage, allowing for zero need for large bulky data storage like massive file cabinets with paper records, where you can just downsize it into a single 4 terabyte hard drive, furthermore preventing accidental data loss with cloud backups and portable hard drives with a copy of the original data whereas physical paper data can easily be damaged and lost. [1]

Any business can benefit from information technology from traditional businesses such as brick and mortar stores, or larger businesses as discussed above resulting in increased innovation and efficiency, but also agricultural businesses, as farmers can make use of IT for production records, research, financial planning, and so on. Making the farmer and any business more efficient than they would be without these advancements in IT. [2]

Describing the risks that might imapct digital projects

The failure of digital projects tends to revolve around funding mismanagement, and time mismanagement, take as an example the case of Star Citizen, a project that has had funding of over $229 million to this date, and has been in production since 2012, yet the game remains in an Alpha state at the time of writing. [4]
Other reasons could be due to network failures if team members are unable to communicate, or lack of skills amongst the individual team members if they lack the skills to perform the required tasks then there will be a resulting bottleneck of tasks being performed, but a build up of tasks needed to be done that are lacking anyone with the skills to perform them. In addition, power failure could be a potential loss of data, and can disrupt workflow. Another potential issue is the chance of having malware corrupt a system, at best, this would cause delays, at worst, loss of data, leading into that would be human error, hopefully a good project would account for human error, having checks in place to prevent human error derailing the projects progress. There’s also the sheer chance of outside intervention of life factors affecting team members ability to work.
